About me

Computer Engineering student at Universidad de Guadalajara (CUCEI).

I build games in Godot and desktop applications with Avalonia UI, both in C#, and I have a strong foundation in C and C++ focused on algorithms.

I also develop backend projects, building APIs in C# along with some frontend work in Next.js and JavaScript.

Outside of code, I enjoy street photography and drawing pixel art.

ProcLot Sim

You can add processes to this app and then start the simulation to see how the processes work.
